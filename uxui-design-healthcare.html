Curriculum Overview

13 Weeks. 480 Hours.
Six Modules to Job-Ready.

The KTCS UX/UI Design curriculum was designed in collaboration with the Fortune 10 Healthcare employer to produce graduates capable of contributing to enterprise design teams from day one. Graduates are proficient across the full UX/UI toolchain.

Figma Adobe XD Mural Tableau HTML CSS
01
UX Foundations
UX principles, design thinking methodology, human-centered design, accessibility standards (WCAG), user research fundamentals, and the role of UX in enterprise product development.
Design Thinking
02
User-Centered Analysis & Conceptual Design
User research methods, persona development, journey mapping, information architecture, card sorting, affinity diagramming, and translating research into design concepts.
Research Methods
03
Science & Art of Effective UI Design
Visual design principles, typography, color theory, layout systems, design systems and component libraries, wireframing, and high-fidelity prototyping in Figma and Adobe XD.
Figma · Adobe XD
04
Agile Product Management & HTML/CSS
Agile methodology, sprint planning, product backlog management, stakeholder communication, HTML structure, CSS styling, and the design-to-development handoff process.
Agile · HTML · CSS
05
Practical Usability Testing
Usability test planning, moderated and unmoderated testing methods, think-aloud protocols, heuristic evaluation, test analysis using Tableau, and synthesizing findings into design recommendations.
Usability · Tableau
06
Capstone & Career Readiness
End-to-end design project from brief to prototype, portfolio development, design critique sessions, professional presentation to employer stakeholders, and apprenticeship transition preparation.
Portfolio · Employer Presentation
