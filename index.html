Federal Alignment

DOL AI Literacy
Framework — TEN 07-25

KTCS programs incorporating AI tools, cloud computing, and emerging technologies are aligned to TEN 07-25 — the U.S. Department of Labor’s AI Literacy Framework issued February 2026. This alignment positions AI-integrated KTCS programs for current and future federal funding eligibility.

Note: The DOL AI Literacy Framework alignment applies to programs where AI tools, prompt engineering, and cloud technologies are part of the curriculum. The DOL Demonstration Grant is a separate and distinct credential.
